About

I’m a Detroit native currently living in Cincinnati, Ohio. For the past 5 years, I have worked in various data science capacities for 84.51 (formerly dunnhumbyUSA), a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Kroger Company. My present role involves building and evaluating the recommendation systems for Kroger’s customer-facing and internal websites and applications.

My undergraduate education in Economics was completed at the University of Notre Dame with one semester spent in London. My graduate education in Statistics (Masters) was completed at Texas A&M University, while working full-time.

My non-technical interests include coffee appreciation, learning, hiking, and supporting the Cincinnati public library system.
